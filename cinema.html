Suntec City

Northpoint City

Level 3, 51 Yishun Central 1, Singapore 768794

Nearest Yishun MRT



Northpoint City is located at the heart of the vibrant Arts and Heritage District. Fitted with state-of-the-art digital projection systems, 3D capabilities and Dolby 7.1 Surround Sound, its 8 halls feature comfortable seats with generous legroom, and regularly plays host to various local and international film screenings and events.
